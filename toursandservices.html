Our Safari Packages

Standard 5-Day Safari Itinerary

Our flagship tour, designed to give you a comprehensive Kruger experience. We can also create tailor-made tours to fit your specific needs and interests.

Day 1: Arrival and Riverside Braai

  • Pickup from Mpumalanga International Airport.
  • Transfer to the scenic Belvedere on the River.
  • Evening braai (barbecue) by the river, inside the Kruger fence (all drinks included).

Day 2: Full Day Game Drive

  • 6:30 am: Breakfast is served.
  • Enter Kruger Park via Malelane Gate in a closed 4x4 for a day of game viewing.
  • Lunch at the picturesque Skukuza Golf Club.
  • Afternoon drinks at Lower Sabie, followed by a drive to Crocodile Bridge.
  • 6:30 pm: Embark on an evening safari in an open game drive vehicle.
  • Dinner will be served inside the park.

Day 3: Journey to a Private Reserve

  • 6:30 am: Breakfast at Belvedere-on-River.
  • Drive through Kruger Park towards Paul Kruger Gate.
  • Mid-morning coffee, tea, and snacks at Tsokwane.
  • Exit at Paul Kruger Gate for lunch in Hoedspruit.
  • Transfer to a private game reserve on the Oliphants River.
  • Enjoy dinner next to the Oliphants River.

Day 4: Bush Walk & Afternoon Drive

  • 6:30 am: Early morning coffee, rusks, and muesli.
  • 8:00 am: A 3-hour guided bush walk with an armed professional guide.
  • Enjoy a unique breakfast in the bush next to the Olifants River.
  • Return to camp on an open vehicle for a light meal.
  • 2:30 pm: Embark on an afternoon game drive.
  • 6:30 pm: Dinner next to the river.

Day 5: Departure

  • Enjoy a final breakfast.
  • Transfer to Kruger Mpumalanga International Airport for your departure.
