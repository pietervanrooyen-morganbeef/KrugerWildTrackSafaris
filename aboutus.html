Our Passionate Team

With a combined 40 years in the hospitality industry, our management team is dedicated to making your safari a once-in-a-lifetime experience. We have a deep love for the African bush and a passion for sharing it with visitors from all corners of the world.

Heystek van Rooyen

Heystek brings decades of experience in tour operations and a profound knowledge of Kruger's wildlife and ecosystems. His expertise ensures that every safari is safe, educational, and perfectly tailored to our guests' interests. He loves sharing stories of the bush and making every game drive an adventure.

Marlise van Rooyen

Marlise is the heart of our hospitality. With her extensive background in managing luxury accommodations, she ensures every aspect of your stay is comfortable and seamless. From the delicious meals to the fine details of your lodging, Marlise's touch turns a great safari into an unforgettable one.

Our Philosophy

We believe that a safari is more than just seeing animals; it's about connecting with nature and creating lasting memories. We are committed to responsible tourism that respects the wildlife and supports local communities. Join us for an authentic and personal journey into the wild heart of Africa.
